It's 2020, a particularly weird year in my lifetime.

Re-starting a web-log is part of my yearly theme, deliberation. I plan this web-log to help me structuring my thoughts, especially when putting ideas out to the internet in a more deliberated way. Blog is out of fashion? I don't care.

I'm currently interested in the world of mechanical keyboards. Initially I thought it's just a simple clackity-clack, boy am I wrong.

It's started with a humble beginning of “ah, my wireless keyboard was left in the office, I should find a replacement.” Now I'm down in a rabbit hole to understand different types of switches that I can choose.

If you happen to be an owner of mechanical keyboards (according to r/mechanicalkeyboards survey some people hoard up to 100 of this thing), recommend me one or just talk to me about it.

On a slightly different topic, I'm currently working as a Design Lead at Traveloka. After years of being an individual contributor designer, I'm now treading the rope of design leadership. If you have a similar journey I'd love to discuss it with you.

I'm currently living in the outskirt of Jakarta, Indonesia. Although, at this point of time the whereabouts of a person might be irrelevant to be able to stay connected.

We've been in a self-quarantine for more than a month now due to a global pandemic caused by COVID-19. Most people stay at home trying to flatten the curve by inhibiting the spread of the virus.

Some of us cope with this situation by playing a suspiciously-timely released game called Animal Crossing. For me, I've been trying to master the Papu's Pyramid track on Crash Team Racing Nitro-Fueled. I swear now I could draw the track without any references from turn-to-turn.

It's 2020, a particularly weird year in my lifetime. I'm sure a lot of people at least agree with it to some extent.
